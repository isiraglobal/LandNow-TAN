Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and enable basic functionality. No personal identity data is stored in the cookies we use.

2. How We Use Cookies

LandNow uses only essential functional cookies. These are strictly necessary for the website to operate — for example, remembering your cookie consent preference so we don't ask you every time you visit. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies that collect personal data.

3. Cookies We Set

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party cookies. Our site does not embed tracking scripts, advertising pixels, or analytics tools that set cookies on your device.

5. Managing Cookies

When you first visit LandNow, a banner appears asking you to Accept or Reject functional cookies. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and refreshing the page. Most browsers also allow you to control cookies through their settings. Note that rejecting essential cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
